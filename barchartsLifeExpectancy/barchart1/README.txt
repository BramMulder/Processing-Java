Description:
Draws a barchart of the life expectancy data (with the life expectancy in years)

Keys: - !NOTE: Choose one of each catergory or nothing will happen

-Categories-
Pick a Year:
1 - 1997/2005
2 - 1997/2000
3 - 2001/2004
4 - 2005/2008
5 - 2007/2010
6 - 2009/2012
7 - 2011/2014

Pick a gender:
v - Female
m - Male

Pick an Age (life expectancy from said age):
z - 20 years
x - 65 years
-end-

/ - Draw the selected data (Start the acutal visualisation)

c - Clear all drawn bars & title from the screen

i - Take a Screenshot and save it to the directory

MouseClick (lmb) - Draw a horizontal line at the Y-position of the mouse
